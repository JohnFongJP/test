Hello, I'm a software engineer originally from Shenzhen, China, currently based in Tokyo, Japan.
Shenzhen is known as one of the most dynamic technology hubs in the world, and growing up in such an environment sparked my early interest in technology and innovation. Today, I work in Tokyo as a developer, focusing on building reliable systems and solving real-world problems through code.
I enjoy exploring new technologies and continuously improving my technical skills as the tech landscape evolves. Living and working in Tokyo also gives me the opportunity to experience different cultures and perspectives, which I believe helps me grow both as an engineer and as a person.
Outside of work, I’m interested in technology trends, productivity tools, and personal development.
